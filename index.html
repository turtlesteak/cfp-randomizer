Curve Fever Pro - Powers Randomizer

Don't know what powers to use?
Bored of your regular combination?
Check this tool out!

What is this?

This is a randomizer of all 30 powers in CFP.
Cicking the button below will roll a pair of powers at random.

How to use:

  1. Click the name of the highest power that you have unlocked.*
  2. This ensures that you will not be rolled a power you do not have access to.
  3. Click the roll button. It's that easy!
*If you do not select your maximum unlocked power, it will assume all 30 are unlocked.

Duplicate powers:

Duplicate powers are 2 of the same power. For example, you roll Zap for both Power 1 and Power 2.
While you will sometimes roll a duplicate, they will never be displayed.
Duplicates will be logged in the console, and a valid roll will be displayed.
